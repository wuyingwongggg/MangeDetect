What is Sarcoptic Mange?

Sarcoptic mange (scabies) is a highly contagious skin disease affecting many wildlife, domestic, and livestock animals. The Sarcoptes scabiei mange mite infects animals, leading to intense pruritis and irritation due to hypersensitivity reactions from the mites and their products, and in wombats, ultimately leads to their death from secondary infections if not treated. This webpage was designed to support wombat carers and treaters to identify sarcoptic mange infections in wombats.
